The Camera

The RaspCam utilizes the Raspberry Pi Camera v2 module
which is connected to the Pi board via the wider ribbon connector cable.
It relies on MotionEye on the front-end and motion on the back-end for functionality.

Learn More

The Google Assistant

The RaspCam recognizes voice commands from the user through
the Google Assistant by means of a USB microphone. It responds to the
commands based on a python script, and outputs through connected speakers.
